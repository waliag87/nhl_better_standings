About these NHL standings

This independent tracker pulls the latest numbers from the official NHL API and ranks every team by point percentage so you can see the true pecking order at a glance. Division leaders, wild card hopefuls, and bubble teams are highlighted, and the optional playoff calculator reveals how much of their remaining schedule each club must win to stay alive.

Bookmark this page for daily NHL standings updates, compare conferences in seconds, and share the link whenever someone asks for the current NHL playoff picture.

  • Eastern and Western conference tables refresh with every data pull.
  • Point-percentage sort keeps teams with games in hand in the right order.
  • Wild card marker and calculator show realistic playoff odds without guesswork.
Enter the estimated points needed to make the playoffs (typically 90-100). The "Req. Pts %" column will appear showing what percentage of remaining points each team needs to reach your threshold.
